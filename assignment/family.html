My Family

My Family

My family consisted of 8 family members. My mother is a fulltime housewife and my father was an Air Traffic Control Manager at Department of Civil Aviation, KLIA.

I am the youngest out of 6 siblings. 3 of my siblings are married and had started their own family. As time past, My family keeps on growing bigger with my nephews and nieces. Although some are married and have their own family, our bond as a family is strong and will never be broken. My family is my treasure in this life and it can never be replaced with luxuries.

Parents

My father's full name is Abdullah Bin Ahmad. He graduated from Sekolah Tuanku Abdul Rahman and graduated from the University of Science Malaysia in Penang.

My mother's full name is NorHayati Binti Talib. My mother was once a teacher before she became a fulltime housewife to dedicate herself to her childrens and husband.

Both of my parents are born and raised in Johor. My parents got married on 11th November 1989 and gave birth to their first child a year after. When i was 10 years old, My father was diagnosed with a heart disease and was hospitalised in National Heart Institute. He passed away a year later due to a heart attack during an event with his co-workers. Although I din't get to spend alot of time with my dad, He is a man that I admire and idolize.
